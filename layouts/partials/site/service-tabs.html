Web Hosting

Our managed hosting service is fairly priced, as you only pay for maintenance. We'll configure and launch your app on trusted services we use ourselves, and you only pay for what you use.

Pay-as-you-Scale

Our hosting service is provided at-cost, and separately managed by trusted providers. For WordPress sites, our recommendation is WPEngine. For modern and specialty sites, our recommendation is Netlify. Pricing is based on usage, so an increase in hosting costs will only occur as your business scales.

Managed Hosting

Our basic managed hosting package includes setup and monitoring of your site instance, with maintenance priced at a discount from our normal hourly support rate. We offer multiple managed options, with one sure to fit the amount of monthly maintenance your site requires.

Unmanaged Hosting

For some of our clients, site updates are infrequent enough to not warrant maintenance. For just the cost of hosting (paid to the provider, not us), we'll monitor your site for uptime. Should there be a need to intervene, it is priced at the normal hourly support rate.
